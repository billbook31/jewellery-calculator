Help & Support

Welcome to the Jewellery Calculator Help section. This tool helps you calculate jewellery value, old gold exchange, and generate quick printable bills.

💍 Common Questions

1. How to add items?
Enter item details and click “Add Item.”

2. How to include old jewellery?
Enter total weight, jalan weight, and rate (optional). The calculator will auto-adjust the net value.

3. How to save or print?
Click “Save & Print” to generate a bill preview. You can also export as PDF or share on WhatsApp.
